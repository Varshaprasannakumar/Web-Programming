Wayanad is a rural district in Kerala state,southwest India.In the east,the Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary is a lush leopards and egrets .In the Ambukuthi Hills to the south,edakkal caves contain ancient petroglyphs,some datir. This area is famous for its large amount of camping and trekking trails, breathtaking waterfalls, caves, bird watching sites, flora, fauna and an overall plethora of magnificent sights. This area has been a tourist favourite over the years.
